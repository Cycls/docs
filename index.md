## Sarya

**Sarya** is a software framework to build, publish, and share your AI powered apps.

> github,discord, links

With Sarya:
- **100% control over your code**: Sarya is a two-tier architecture. Maintain your app logic and data on your infrastructure, while Sarya manages the UI rendering.
- **Generative UI**: intuitive approach to quickly build powerful UIs that integrate seamlessly with your AI logic. No frontend code required.
- **Instant sharing & monetization**: Instantly share your customer-facing AI apps with Sarya for monetization and broader audience reach.
- **Data & analytics**: Measure app's performance. Use data to understand user trends and finetune AI for better results.
- **Cross-platform**: Sarya currently is web-based. Mobile support for iOS and Android is underway. Sarya chat will also be accessible on apps like WhatsApp and Discord.
- **Multimodal I/O**: Starting with textual input, the platform will expand to support voice, image, and video data inputs.
